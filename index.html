What is SOSassist?

SOSassist is an user-friendly app that connects the people with emergency needs to the nearest hospital.

Our mission is to empower individuals during emergencies by providing a life-saving mobile app that seamlessly connects them to nearby hospitals, skilled paramedics, and reliable ambulance services.
